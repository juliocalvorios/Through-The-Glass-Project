# Ambient Sounds

Place your sound files here. Required files:

## Weather Sounds
- `rain.mp3` - Gentle rain ambiance
- `storm.mp3` - Heavy storm with wind
- `thunder.mp3` - Thunder crack (single sound, not looping)
- `wind.mp3` - Gentle wind
- `snow-wind.mp3` - Soft wind for snowy conditions

## Ambient Sounds
- `fireplace.mp3` - Cozy fireplace crackling (plays at night)
- `birds.mp3` - Birds chirping (plays during clear day)
- `crickets.mp3` - Cricket sounds (plays at night)
- `aurora-ambient.mp3` - Ethereal ambient sound for aurora

## Recommended Sources (Free)
- freesound.org
- pixabay.com/sound-effects
- mixkit.co/free-sound-effects

## Tips
- Use MP3 format for best compatibility
- Keep files small (< 2MB each)
- Ensure sounds loop seamlessly
- Thunder should be a short, single crack sound
